[PROMISE TRACKING] TSLA: full self driving

VERDICT: Still Pending (confidence: high)

From Q1 2019 through Q4 2021, Tesla management consistently discussed plans, pilots, and ongoing development of Full 
Self-Driving (FSD) technology without ever confirming full-scale delivery. Early statements in 2019 set ambitious goals 
such as 1 million robo taxis by 2020, but subsequent quarters reveal cautious optimism, incremental feature rollouts, 
and beta testing rather than full deployment. Despite progress and expanded beta programs, management repeatedly 
emphasized that FSD was not yet complete or fully functional, with safety and regulatory approval still outstanding as 
of the latest quarter.
