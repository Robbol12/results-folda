The consistency between the guidance and the final reported figures, especially those sourced from filings, reinforces the reliability of management’s revenue forecasts.

Conclusion:

The consistent application of ASC 606 revenue recognition and transparent disclosures support confidence in the reported revenue figures. Overall, the evidence indicates a strong and credible revenue growth trajectory for the company.
