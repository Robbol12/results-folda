[WHAT CHANGED SINCE LAST QUARTER] TSLA: production ramp

Quarters compared: TSLA_Q3_2025, TSLA_Q4_2025
Change detected: True

full self-driving. However, in Q4 2025, the focus shifted to ramping production at all factories, with a specific 
mention of constraints on the battery pack front and the need for more compute to scale Optimus.
