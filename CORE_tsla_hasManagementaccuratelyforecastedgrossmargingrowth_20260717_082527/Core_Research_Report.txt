Despite some fluctuations, the overall trajectory showed an upward trend in gross margin performance.

Forecasting credibility: High
Management consistently met or exceeded their guidance on gross margin performance throughout the period, demonstrating a high level of forecasting credibility.

Trends:

Key concerns:
  - No major inconsistencies or red flags were identified in the forecasting of gross margins.

Conclusion:
Management has demonstrated a high level of accuracy in forecasting gross margin growth or decline.
