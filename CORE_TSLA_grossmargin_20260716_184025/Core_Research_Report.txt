Forecasting credibility: Low
Management consistently provided guidance on achieving mid-20% gross margins for the automotive business but failed to meet these targets in each reporting period.

Trends:
  - Decline in gross margins for automotive business
  - Struggle to meet mid-20% gross margin target
  - Efforts to improve efficiencies and stabilize margins

Recurring themes/risks:
  - Consistent shortfall in gross margin targets
  - Impact of price cuts on gross margins
  - Efficiency improvements not keeping pace with cuts

Key concerns:
  - Management's inability to accurately forecast and achieve gross margin targets
  - Potential impact of external factors like tariffs on future margins

Conclusion:
The company's gross margin performance for the automotive business consistently fell short of management's guidance, indicating a low forecasting credibility. The decline in gross margins over each reporting period suggests ongoing challenges in achieving profitability targets. Despite efforts to improve efficiencies and stabilize margins, the company struggled to meet its mid-20% gross margin target, raising concerns about its ability to effectively manage costs and pricing strategies.
