Over the periods from Q1 2021 through Q4 2021, management consistently emphasized their commitment to achieving and sustaining industry-leading operating margins. Early guidance was optimistic but vague, focusing on comparable margins in storage and vehicles and progress over several quarters. By Q3 2021, the company reported exceeding its long-term operating margin targets, achieving just under 15%, which was confirmed again in Q4 2021 with margins reaching mid-teens and described as the highest in the industry. The narrative highlighted margin improvements driven by volume growth, operating leverage, and overhead cost management.

Forecasting credibility: High
Management provided three guidance/actual pairs related to operating margins, all of which were met or exceeded. The actuals reported in Q3 and Q4 2021 confirmed that the company not only met but surpassed their long-term margin targets, demonstrating strong forecasting credibility. The consistency between guidance and results over multiple quarters supports a high rating for forecasting reliability.

Conclusion:
The company's margin performance showed a clear upward trajectory throughout 2021, with management's guidance consistently aligned with or exceeded by actual results. Operating margins improved to mid-teens percentages, supported by volume growth and effective cost control. The evidence indicates that the company has strong control over its margin drivers and credible forecasting practices, making margin expansion a reliable aspect of its financial profile.
