Longitudinal Summary

Over the reported periods from Q1 2025 through Q1 2026, the company consistently highlighted the seasonality of its operating cash flows, which are weighted toward the second half of the year. Actual operating cash flows showed a decline year-over-year in each quarter of 2025, primarily driven by decreases in net cash flows related to margin deposits supporting the hedging strategy. Despite these fluctuations, management's full-year adjusted free cash flow before growth for 2025 exceeded the midpoint of guidance, and they provided confident forward guidance for 2026 and 2027 free cash flow per share. The company also accelerated share repurchases in early 2026, reflecting strong free cash flow yield and cash generation. Throughout, the company maintained detailed disclosures on cash flow drivers, including margin deposits, environmental allowances, and insurance proceeds.
